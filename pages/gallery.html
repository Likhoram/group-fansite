Our group chose to create a Home Alone webpage because it’s a classic holiday movie. With the holidays coming up, we wanted to bring back some nostalgia and celebrate the fun and clever moments of Kevin’s adventures.These scenes are what make Home Alone so memorable. From Kevin’s fiery traps and his classic aftershave moment to the spider scene, each one shows how clever and brave he becomes. It’s fun to watch him turn fear into confidence and take control of his own adventure.
